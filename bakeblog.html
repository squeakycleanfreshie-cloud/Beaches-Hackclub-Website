Bakebuild

Fri 3 Jul, 2026

So today after school, everybody met at manly library. We promptly got started on creating our cookie cutters. Because it was our first session, I explained to everyone about the basics of hack club - how it works, about YSWS programs ect. Then I lead the session, explaining to everyone how to make the cookie cutters. First everybody designed them on paper, scetching multiple illerations of what we wanted, then we used onshape, utilizing the extrude and spline tool mainly to complete them. Once everyone had finished creating at least one cutter we submited them online, packed up and walked over to butterboy, a local cookie company that create hot, delicious fudgy cookies. Ben got a cinnamon bun like myself, Quinn got a apple crumble and Druhv got a kinder Bueno cookie, We all sat and ate our cookies while I handed out the certificates. This was a very fun session.
